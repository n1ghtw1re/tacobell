Nutritional Compliance Mandate

Ensuring Citizen Wellness Through Standardized Sustenance Protocols.

Adherence to San Angeles Health Protocol 8.1

Taco Bell operates in strict accordance with the mandated nutritional guidelines established by the San Angeles Health and Wellness Council. Our commitment ensures that all sustenance provided contributes positively to citizen well-being and societal harmony, eliminating historically detrimental dietary elements.

Prohibited Constituent Elements

To guarantee optimal physiological function and emotional equilibrium, the following agents have been eliminated from all approved Taco Bell sustenance formulations:

  • Sodium Chloride (Common Salt) & Related Sodium Compounds
  • Refined Sugars & Artificial Sweeteners
  • Capsaicin & All Forms of Chemical/Organic Spices
  • Animal-Derived Muscle Tissue (Meat/Poultry/Fish)
  • Saturated & Trans-Unsaturated Fats (Including Dairy Butter)
  • Caffeine & Other Non-Prescribed Stimulants
  • Gluten & Other Identified Potential Allergens (Prophylactically)

Our formulations prioritize nutrient density, digestibility, and baseline metabolic support through precisely controlled synthesized ingredients.
